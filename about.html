About Me

Hello! I'm Michael Pignatelli, a computer science student at the University of Pennsylvania. I specialize in computer vision, AI, and creative software solutions. I'm also pursuing a second major in cognitive science.

I serve as a Head Teaching Assistant for CIS 1100, helping ~300 students each semester. I manage a team of 40+ TAs, collaborate with faculty to update curriculum and author exams, and lead weekly staff meetings. I teach data structures, recursion, OOP, functional programming, and pandas.

Most recently, I interned on the Platform Team at Ava Labs (AvalancheGo): implemented and productionized 4 gRPC APIs (+7.5k LoC) to replace legacy JSON-RPC, enabling strongly-typed communication for ~1,800 node operators.
